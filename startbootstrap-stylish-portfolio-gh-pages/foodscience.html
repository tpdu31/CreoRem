Food Science

Our aims

We have created a website to provide University Students (but not only) with quick and easy to make meals for breakfast, lunch and dinner, with the aim of promoting a healthy lifestyle. The recipes we have chosen constitute vegan, gluten-free, vegetarian and meat meals. These meals are cost-effective, healthy and delicious!

Vegan - The New Food Trend?

More and more people are becoming vegan, and has shown to improve weight loss and lower cholesterol levels. About 1 in 100 people have celiac disease caused by gluten causing abdominal pain and bloating.

Healthy and delicious?

Our recipes include more fruits and vegetables, as well as beans to provide a source of dietary fibre and proteins. Healthy eating helps lower the risk of heart disease and maintain healthy blood pressure.
